Our Services

"Your Dream Is Our Future"

It is important to have strong and reliable construction services to provide top quality housing and commercial centers. Some of the people hire normal labor for their construction services and have to monitor them 24/7 and they still mess up your projects. Construction projects cost a considerable amount and no one can afford to get their projects messed up. Hence you need construction professionals to bring your dreams to life.

CGDI group offers services related to building, designing, and creating innovative products according to the new trends of the construction market. CGDI group is divided into 4 categories: Construction, Architectural, Engineering, and IT Solutions.

Construction

CGDI is offering various construction services ranging from planning, structure analyzing, constructing, and execution and maintaining of construction services.

• Design-Build Solutions
• Structural to General Construction
• Fit outs and Renovations

Architectural

Our architects also provide a wide variety of additional services including feasibility studies, architectural programming and project management.

• Concept Design Development
• Construction Documents
• Interior Designs
• Landscaping


Engineering

Our engineering team provides multidisciplinary consulting related to the design, simulation, evaluation and investigation needed to safely construct or manufacture the asset.

• Consultation, research and reports
• Construction services
• Engineering support services

IT Solutions

Our IT solutions services allow businesses to create, manage and optimize information and business processes including network and cybersecurity.

• Structural Network Cabling and Configuration
• Surveillance System and Commissioning
• Fiber Optic Deployment
• Web/System Design and Development
• Telecommunication Services [Internet. Radio. Antenna etc.]
• Access Control

About Us

ContractorsGuild Development Inc. or CGDI is a group of contractors from different construction companies (ArmRock Constructions, J.O. Cayabat Construction Services), architectural firm (Scaled Inc), and IT solution company (DIKWIS Network and Internet Solutions, KreativesPH). Formed together to engaged in General Construction and other allied activities from small to larger scale including buildings, manufacturing plants, roads and highways, etc. Equipped with professional skills, experience and sucessful projects, effective risk management team, and the commitment to safety and security is the full arsenal of CGDI in the design-build industry that can exceed the client's expectation.
